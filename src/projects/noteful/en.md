Noteful is a mobile app concept designed entirely in Figma, created to solve the core problem most note apps ignore: they try to do everything and end up overwhelming the user who just wants to write something down and find it again. The design reduces the experience to two workflows — freeform notes and shopping lists — unified under a flat, single-surface architecture. Every screen, from onboarding through empty states to editing, was designed at native resolution with auto-layout components ready for direct developer handoff.
