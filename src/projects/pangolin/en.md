Pangolin is a responsive interactive dashboard prototype developed to serve as a visual and functional mockup for a client presentation, helping them envision their future internal admin panel. The challenge was demonstrating real dynamic data handling and polished UI within a strict showcase deadline, working in a compact team of three developers. The prototype successfully won over the client and was structured as a modular codebase ready for a production team to build upon.
