Portfolio v5 is the latest iteration of my personal online presence — a fully custom-designed and developed site built solo from Figma concept through to production deployment. The challenge was creating a site that felt more mature and content-rich than previous versions, solved by introducing a dark theme, TypeScript throughout, and a file-based markdown content system for projects and blog posts. A webhook-integrated contact form was added to streamline inbound job opportunities directly to Discord.
