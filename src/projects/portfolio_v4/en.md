Portfolio v4 is a complete redesign and rebuild of my personal web presence, conceived and executed entirely by me — from initial Figma concepts through to the final SvelteKit implementation. The challenge was creating a site that felt distinctly personal without sacrificing professionalism, resolved through a clean white theme and a handwritten name signature as the central branding element. Every line of design and code was written solo, with a focus on performance and long-term maintainability.
